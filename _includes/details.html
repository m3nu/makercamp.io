Organizational details

What's included

We provide you with everything you need to efficiently work on your product. Our program includes:

  • Airport transfer, flight recommendations
  • Volunteer team as local guides
  • Business visa to China, letter of invitation
  • Accomodation in a strategic location (single, double or suite)
  • Access to workshop and PCB printing facility (charged at cost)
  • Shared driver and courier for collecting parts
  • Local secretarial support
  • Research team to find producers and factories

Approximate cost

Our team consists of volunteers, who share your passion for innovative hardware products. Organizational services provided by our local sourcing partner are charged at cost.

We estimate the monthly cost at USD 1200, when using a single room.
