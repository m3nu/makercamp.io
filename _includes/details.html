Organizational details

What's included

We take the pain out of sourcing from China, by providing you a structured and and pleasant environment. Our program includes:

  • Airport transfer, flight recommendations
  • Team with a variety of skill to push your business
  • Regular demos, dinners and networking to help you reach goals and stay motivated.
  • Business visa to China, letter of invitation
  • Accommodation in a strategic location (single or double room)
  • Local secretarial support
  • Research team to find producers and factories
  • Work with local engineering students to improve CAD drafts or graphic design
  • Workshops on crowd-funding, sourcing, marketing and other topics (or run your own workshop)

Approximate cost

Our team consists of volunteers, who share your passion for innovative hardware products. Organizational services provided by our local sourcing partner are charged at cost.

We estimate the monthly cost at $1500/month or $50 per day, when using a double room. This breaks down like this: 50-60% hotels, 20% Chinese support staff, 10-20% transport, 10% for group dinners and 10% for various items, like SIM cards, office expenses, etc.

Once you are accepted into the program, we require a 50% deposit to be transferred to our Chinese organizer. They will use it for hotel deposits and other expenses.
