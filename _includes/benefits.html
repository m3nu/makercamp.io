Crowdfunding Accelerator.

Our mission is to increase the rate of success for crowdfunding projects by bringing a group of motivated people together at the same time right in the heart of where new things get made.

We believe having 20 projects and a clear schedule will let us learn together, solve roadbloacks, negotiate better pricing, and reduce the risk.

More than that, having a smart group of people give you feedback on your videos, your photos, your text can really help you polish your marketing.
